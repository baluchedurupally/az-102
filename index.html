Use on iPhone

  1. Host this folder on Netlify, GitHub Pages, or Vercel.
  2. Open the site in Safari on your iPhone.
  3. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen.
  4. The app opens full screen and works offline after the first load.

Local test from laptop

cd ai102-iphone-pwa-v3
python3 -m http.server 8080 --bind 0.0.0.0

Then open http://YOUR_LAPTOP_IP:8080 in Safari on the same Wi‑Fi network.
